5.108.1. Issues

5.108.1.1. Mockito Maintenance Burden

1
The module makes heavy use of Mockito in order to mock dependency classes during tests. Unfortunately, this sometimes results in tests with hundreds of lines of setup code, as all dependencies must be laboriously mocked one method at a time. We have made some inroads towards replacing these with hand-written fake classes, or even just using the real implementations of dependencies for tests. Even so, the test suite currently represents a large maintenance burden whenever any kind of refactoring is performed.
2
Issue class: COMPLEX. Severity: Major.
